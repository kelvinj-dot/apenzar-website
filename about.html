The Etymology

Apenzar [ah-pen-zar]

From the Asturian dialect; a verb meaning "to start" or "to initiate." Etymologically rooted in the concept of pettia (fragmentation)—the act of breaking a massive whole into actionable pieces to begin a complex task.

This is our methodology. We fragment complex market barriers to bridge the gap between Institutional Capital and Local Capacity. We do not guess; we calculate.

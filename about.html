The Etymology

Apenzar [ah-pen-zar]

From the Asturian dialect; a verb meaning "to start" or "to initiate." Etymologically rooted in the concept of pettia (fragmentation)—breaking a massive whole into actionable pieces to begin a complex task.

Our name defines our function. We do not just "consult" on the whole; we fragment complex market barriers into executable pieces. We initiate the flow of capacity to capital.

Est. 2014 • Beverly Hills, CA

01. The Discipline

Algorithmic Management Consulting

Traditional consulting relies on human intuition and qualitative reports. Algorithmic Management Consulting is the displacement of intuition by computation.

The Placement Tender

To ensure the integrity of our Purchasing Blocks, access is strictly controlled. We utilize a Placement Tender (Sealed-Bid Auction) to rigorously filter for SME quality and verify operational capacity.

01

The Invitation

This is the currency. You purchase Invitation Options which grant you the "Right to Invite." You send a unique code to the SME.

02

The Sealed Bid

The SME registers and submits a Capacity Bid. They compete for a slot in the protocol (e.g., a seat in The Syndicate's Purchasing Block).

03

The Audit

Apenzar verifies the "Strike Group" (Finalists). We ensure the SME meets the criteria for the Principal's Order.

04

The Settlement

The Principal executes the contract. The Bounty is released to you immediately upon clearance.
